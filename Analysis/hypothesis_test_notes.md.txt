# Hypothesis Testing Notes

## Business Question

Does the new onboarding campaign improve Paid Conversion Rate?

## Metric Tested

Paid Conversion Rate

## Null Hypothesis (H0)

There is no statistically significant difference between the Control and Treatment groups.

## Alternative Hypothesis (H1)

The Treatment group has a higher Paid Conversion Rate than the Control group.

## Test Type

One-tailed hypothesis test.

## Significance Level

α = 0.05

## Decision Rule

* If p-value < 0.05 → Reject H0.
* If p-value ≥ 0.05 → Fail to Reject H0.

## Business Interpretation

The Treatment group showed stronger user activation and engagement. However, guardrail metrics such as Support Ticket Rate increased noticeably. Therefore, although the campaign appears promising, a phased rollout with additional monitoring is recommended.

## Conclusion

Continue testing before a full-scale launch while monitoring support tickets and customer satisfaction.
