# Executive Recommendation Memo

## Executive Summary

The onboarding experiment compared the existing onboarding experience with a redesigned onboarding campaign. The Treatment group demonstrated higher engagement, stronger landing page visits, improved trial starts, and faster conversion.

## North Star Metric

Paid Conversion Rate

## KPI Tree Summary

The campaign focused on increasing Paid Conversion Rate through improvements in:

* User Acquisition
* User Activation
* User Engagement

Guardrail metrics included:

* Refund Rate
* Support Ticket Rate
* Average Days to Convert

## Experiment Results

Positive Findings:

* Higher Landing Page Visit Rate
* Higher Trial Start Rate
* Higher Engagement Score
* Faster Average Days to Convert

Risk Identified:

* Increased Support Ticket Rate

## Recommendation

**Recommendation: Continue Testing with a Phased Rollout**

The campaign demonstrates positive business impact but introduces additional customer support demand. A phased rollout allows validation across larger user segments while reducing business risk.

## Risks

* Increased customer support workload
* Potential onboarding confusion
* Higher operational costs

## Next Steps

* Investigate causes of support tickets
* Monitor refund trends
* Run the experiment for a longer period
* Re-evaluate statistical significance before full deployment
